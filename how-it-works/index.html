What to expect

What shapes your offer.

Every lot is different. Instead of quoting a generic range, here are the six factors our AI weighs to price your lot before a human deal lead signs the final number.

Price drivers

The six factors our AI weighs on every offer

01

Brand recognition

Known brands have deeper buyer demand. Demand widens the offer window.

02

Condition clarity

Honest, documented condition lets us price tighter. Surprises compress the offer.

03

Manifest completeness

SKU, quantity, MSRP, location, condition. More fields filled means a faster audit and a stronger number.

04

Volume

Single pallets, full truckloads, and multi-site lots run on different logistics lanes and price grids.

05

Market timing

Current-generation or in-season stock recovers more. We benchmark against live channel comps the day we quote.

06

Channel flexibility

Broader resale rights (export, retail, B2B) open more exit paths. Brand-restricted buys are still possible, with a written commitment.

Why we don't quote generic ranges

A 2024 Apple laptop lot and a 2019 off-brand tablet lot can both be “returns.” Their real recovery values are nowhere close.

Our AI weighs the six factors above against ~40,000 live market comps per offer, then a human deal lead signs the final number. That is how you get a firm price, not a range.
