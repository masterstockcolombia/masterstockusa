Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

01Do you take title directly, or match us with another buyer?

Yes. We take title, we wire from our own US bank account, and we move the stock. We don't list your inventory or shop it around.

02How does the 48-hour offer work?

Send a manifest. We audit within 48 hours and return a firm, written price. Non-binding on your side. You accept or decline.

03How is the payment structured?

Paid at pickup. Balance released after 7-day inspection window. All wires originate from our US Mercury Treasury account.

04What items do you not buy?

We don't buy FDA-regulated items (food, drugs, medical devices, cosmetics with claims), firearms, hazmat, or live goods. Everything else is open.

05What format do you need the manifest in?

A spreadsheet (Excel or CSV), one row per SKU. At minimum: SKU, description, quantity, unit cost or MSRP, and condition. We share a template if you don't have one.

06What does it cost me to sell to you?

Nothing. Zero fees, zero listing costs. We're your buyer, not a marketplace. Decline the offer, owe nothing.

07How fast do I get paid?

Partial payment at pickup, balance within 7 days of inspection sign-off. End-to-end: 10 business days typical.

08Can we sign an NDA before I share the manifest?

Yes. We sign mutual NDAs for any manifest you request. Request a template from legal@masterstockunitedstates.com.

09Where do you pickup from?

50-state pickup. Our main hubs are in California, New Jersey, and Texas. We cover any US location.

10Do you do one-off deals or only recurring?

Both. One-time clear-outs and ongoing monthly overstock programs. Pricing improves with volume and recurrence.

11Will my brand end up on eBay or Amazon?

Not without your consent. Channel restrictions are built into every purchase agreement. SKU-level controls available on request.

12Is there a minimum lot size?

No minimum. We evaluate anything from a few pallets to full trailer loads. Larger lots get better per-unit pricing.

01/03 Offer Structure
Full-Lot

One firm price. The entire manifest. Fastest close.

How it works

One wire at closing. We take title on everything (A-stock, B-stock, and residual) in a single transaction.

Tradeoff

Mixed lots price conservatively. Premium SKUs get averaged with the rest.

Response window

48 hours firm, good for 5 business days.

Settlement

100% at pickup. Balance confirmation after 7-day intake inspection.

Best for

Suppliers who want speed, certainty, and zero residual sitting on their dock.

02/03 Offer Structure
Selective Buy

Premium per-unit for what matches our network.

How it works

SKU-level review. We make a firm offer only on the units that match our resale channels, at premium rates.

Tradeoff

Residual stays with you. You keep what we don't take, or dispose separately.

Response window

72 hours firm. SKU-level analysis takes longer to prepare.

Settlement

100% at pickup on the selected SKUs. Clean bill of sale per-SKU.

Best for

Suppliers with a high-quality core and a plan for the residual.

03/03 Offer Structure
Floor + Upside

Guaranteed minimum + 50/50 on recovery above benchmark.

How it works

Firm floor paid at closing. We resell through our network. Any recovery above the agreed benchmark is split 50/50 with you.

Tradeoff

Full settlement takes 60–90 days depending on sell-through.

Response window

48h for floor offer + benchmark. Full structure in the signed agreement.

Settlement

Floor at pickup. Monthly recovery reports. Upside wired as sales clear.

Best for

Mixed-quality lots. Highest total return in most cases.
