You pay on release, we both stay risk-free

You wire 100% of the FOB price, we release the Bill of Lading, the cargo is yours. We hold the title the whole way, so you are always paying against cargo that is ready and verified. The lowest price on the platform, and the cleanest way to start.

You pay only when the cargo is released and verified. We hold the title until your wire clears, so you are never paying into the void. It is also the cheapest price on the platform.

A valid legal entity and a clean compliance check. No US credit history, no deposit. One clean cycle here unlocks the next rung.

Cycle 2 · Net-15 (30% down + 70% in 15 days)

Receive the cargo, pay most of it after

You pay 30% to confirm, we release the cargo, and you settle the remaining 70% within 15 days, once it is moving in your market. You stop fronting your full capital on every order.

Cargo firstPay 70% in 15 daysEarned in 2 clean cycles

Two clean wire cycles. The relationship earns the terms, not the paperwork.

One transaction covers sourcing (founder-verified, no scams), financing, and CIF logistics to your port. The terms are working capital, built into the price (roughly +1.5% vs. paying cash), smaller than the capital they free.

Cycle 3+ · Net-30 (open account)

Sell first, pay in 30 days

You receive the full order and pay 30 days later, with the goods already selling in your market. The USD credit line your bank will not give you, built into the purchase with no banking paperwork.
