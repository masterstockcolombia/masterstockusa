FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

How do you actually vet a lot?

We source straight from US brands, distributors, and 3PLs. Before a lot goes on the platform we walk the manifest line by line, confirm condition grading with the supplier, and reject anything that looks re-listed or repackaged. If we wouldn't buy it, you won't see it.

Will the lot match the manifest at pickup?

That's the whole point. The manifest you approve is the manifest that ships. If something is off at pickup, we make it right or you don't pay.

How do Net-30 terms work?

Once you're onboarded and credit-reviewed, you can take a lot on Net-30. You pick it up, you have 30 days to pay. Prepay and pay-at-pickup are also options on every lot.

Do you handle the export?

No. You take title in the US and route the freight yourself, or we'll introduce you to forwarders we work with. We don't act as importer of record abroad.

Do I need an account to see what's available?

Yes. Live inventory is gated so suppliers stay protected. Request access and we'll review your profile within two business days.

Is there a minimum order?

Minimums are set per lot and shown on the listing. Most lots are sized for a full or partial container.
