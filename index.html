FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

How do you actually vet a lot?

Every lot passes a physical inspection at the origin warehouse. Manifests are reconciled against the real count. Category, condition, grade and quantity are all confirmed before we list it.

What payment terms do you offer?

Cash on pickup, or Net-30 after onboarding and credit review.

Do you ship internationally?

US-domestic pickup by default. We coordinate export logistics on request (you handle the import side).

What categories are available?

Apparel returns, consumer electronics, home and kitchen, toys, health and beauty, tools, sporting goods. The mix shifts week to week.

What's the minimum order?

Lot-level. No SKU-level MOQ. Smallest lots start around $3K to $5K cost. Largest go above $250K.

Can I inspect before buying?

Yes for committed buyers. You can schedule a warehouse visit or a video walkthrough once your access is approved.

What happens if the lot doesn't match the manifest?

Documented discrepancies above 5% trigger a pro-rated credit. Resolved within 7 business days.

Do you compete with my customers?

No. We don't sell to end consumers and we don't run retail or marketplace channels.

How fast can I get my first lot?

Speed depends on inventory fit and your credit review. We aim to move approved buyers to their first transaction as soon as a matching lot is open.

Why not just buy from a liquidator directly?

You can. We exist for buyers who want vetted manifests, consistent supply, and Net-30 without chasing 8 different sellers.

Want a seat in the first buyer cohort?

Send the request form. We read every profile by hand and reply within one business day. Approved buyers get access to vetted lots as inventory comes online.
