What is MasterStock

The catalog adapts to you, not the other way around.

Auctions push you whatever is open. Brokers pass through whatever they hold. We do the opposite: we match US liquidation pallets to each buyer's sell-through, defect rate, and SKU velocity.

  • Curated, not listed. Every lot is scored against your buyer profile before it ever reaches the catalog.
  • Aligned on outcomes. If your lot doesn't sell, we lose your next order. So we screen hard before listing.
  • Every transaction sharpens the next. Your sell-through history compounds. By the third pallet, the match fits your channels better than any auction could.
