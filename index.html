FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

How are lots vetted?

Every lot on the MasterStock platform comes from direct supplier relationships with US brands, distributors, and 3PLs. We review each manifest at the SKU level, validate condition grading, and exclude lots that fail our sourcing standards. No re-listed pallets, no mystery boxes.

What payment terms do you offer to buyers?

MasterStock offers Net-30 payment terms to qualified Tier-1 buyers, subject to onboarding and credit review. Standard prepay and pay-at-pickup options are also available on every lot.

Do you ship internationally?

MasterStock operates on US-domestic pickup. Buyers coordinate their own export logistics, or we can refer freight forwarders we work with. We do not act as the importer of record in destination countries.

What categories are available?

Apparel and footwear, electronics, home and kitchen, toys and games, health and beauty (non-regulated), tools, sporting goods, office products, and pet supplies. We do not list FDA-regulated items, alcohol, tobacco, or firearms.

Do I need an account to see inventory?

Yes. Inventory is gated behind buyer onboarding to protect supplier confidentiality and ensure fit. Request access and we will review your profile within two business days.

Is there a minimum order?

We work with serious wholesale buyers. Order minimums are set per lot and are visible on each listing inside the platform. Most lots fit container or partial-container quantities.
