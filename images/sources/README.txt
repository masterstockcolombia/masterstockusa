SPECS
-----
- Format: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or white

- Width: up to 400px max
- Aspect ratio: horizontal wordmark works best


LEGAL - NOMINATIVE FAIR USE
----------------------------
Using a news outlet's logo to attribute an article you're citing is generally
protected under "nominative fair use" doctrine in US trademark law, provided:

1. The source is identified for a factual purpose (not advertising endorsement)
2. The use is limited to what's necessary to identify the source
3. You don't suggest the outlet sponsors/endorses MasterStock
4. Each card clearly links to the source's real article

The current layout ticks all four boxes. Outlets EXPECT to be cited with
their logo when their journalism is referenced.

If you want to be extra safe, add a small disclaimer to the footer:
"Logos and trademarks belong to their respective owners. References are
for editorial attribution under nominative fair use."
